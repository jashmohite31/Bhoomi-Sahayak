    Succession / Legal Heir Amendment Petition

    Revenue Court & Land Office · Government Registry

    As per the Land Revenue Code, modifications to the registered family tree (succession register) cannot be performed online. You must file a petition with supporting legal certificates. Your petition will be reviewed by the Tehsildar / Land Revenue Officer.

    ⚠️ Warning: Filing false declarations in land records is a punishable offence under Section 199 and 200 of the Indian Penal Code.
